Indian Art And Handicraft

India's art heritage is as diverse as its people. Every corner of the country has a unique art form,some that have withstood the test of time and prospered in recent years. But the sad reality is that the different types of art in India are facing the brunt of modernization and are slowly shifting into the category of dying art forms of India.Leaving behind these Indian artworks means forgetting a part of our history. IndianOpedia is here to make efforts to preserve these art forms in India.
